Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does MAMA LAUNDARY offer?

+

We offer a complete range of laundry and dry cleaning services including professional washing, expert ironing, premium dry cleaning, wash & fold service, pickup & delivery, wedding dress care, household items cleaning, corporate services, express service, and basic alterations.

How does your pickup and delivery service work?

+

Our pickup and delivery service is designed for your convenience. Simply call us at 07031339900 or WhatsApp us to schedule a pickup at your preferred time. We'll collect your items, process them with our signature mother's touch care, and deliver them back to you clean and fresh. We offer flexible scheduling to fit your busy lifestyle.

What is your typical turnaround time?

+

Our standard turnaround time is 2-3 business days for regular laundry services and 3-5 business days for dry cleaning. We also offer express service for urgent needs with same-day or next-day completion. Turnaround times may vary during peak seasons or for specialty items requiring extra care.

How do you handle different fabric types?

+

We have extensive experience with all fabric types, from delicate silks to sturdy cottons. Each garment is carefully inspected and sorted by fabric type and care requirements. We use appropriate cleaning methods, temperatures, and products for each specific fabric to ensure optimal results while preserving the integrity and longevity of your garments.

Do you offer stain removal services?

+

Yes! We specialize in stain removal and have saved countless garments from stubborn stains. Our experienced team pre-treats stains before cleaning and uses specialized techniques and products to remove even the toughest stains. While we can't guarantee 100% stain removal on all items, our success rate is very high.

How much do your services cost?

+

Our pricing varies depending on the type of service, garment type, and quantity. We believe in transparent, fair pricing and provide personalized quotes based on your specific needs. Please contact us at 07031339900 or visit our contact page to request a detailed quote. We offer competitive rates with exceptional quality and service.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?

+

Yes, we are committed to environmental responsibility. We use eco-friendly cleaning products and solvents that are safe for both your garments and the environment. Our dry cleaning process uses environmentally safe solvents, and our washing detergents are biodegradable and gentle on fabrics while being tough on stains.

What areas do you serve for pickup and delivery?

+

We currently serve the local metropolitan area and surrounding neighborhoods. Delivery areas may vary, and additional charges may apply for locations outside our standard service area. Please call us at 07031339900 to confirm if we serve your location and to discuss any applicable delivery fees.

Do you offer corporate or bulk services?

+

Absolutely! We provide corporate laundry services for businesses including restaurants, hotels, offices, and medical facilities. We offer competitive bulk pricing, regular pickup schedules, and customized service plans to meet your business needs. Contact us to discuss your corporate laundry requirements.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

+

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, please contact us immediately. We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you to resolve any issues. In most cases, we'll re-clean the item at no additional charge or provide appropriate compensation.

How should I prepare my clothes for pickup?

+

Simply gather your items and check all pockets for personal belongings like coins, pens, tissues, etc. We're not responsible for items left in pockets. If you have specific instructions for certain garments or stains to point out, please let our pickup team know. No need to sort by color or fabric type - we'll handle that for you!

Do you provide hangers and garment bags?

+

Yes, we provide complimentary hangers for items that need to be hung, and protective garment bags for dry cleaned items. If you prefer to use your own hangers, please let us know during pickup. We also offer eco-friendly reusable garment bags for regular customers who want to reduce waste.

Laundry Care Tips

👕

Check Pockets

Always check pockets before bringing clothes to us. Items left in pockets can damage both your garment and our equipment.

🏷️

Read Care Labels

Point out care labels to our team, especially for special or delicate items. This helps us provide the best possible care.

🩸

Treat Stains Early

The sooner you bring stained items to us, the better chance we have of complete stain removal. Don't try home remedies first.

⏰

Plan Ahead

For special events, bring your garments to us well in advance. This allows time for proper care and any necessary re-cleaning.
